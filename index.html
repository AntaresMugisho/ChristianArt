Biography

About Christian Rukeza

Christian Rukeza (born in 2003) is a Congolese artist based in Bujumbura/Burundi, who is known for his hyper realistic pencil and charcoal portraits. He is passionate about creating art that inspires, motivates and edifies, was o change the world positively.

The journey

Christian began drawing since he was young and whole neighborhood knows that he is an artist and even at school his teacher had no problems when it came to drawing on the board for others. At the age 17 that Christian decided to laugh into art and pursue a professional career. He began taking the basic drawing course at a training center in his neighborhood and at the end the training he decided to be self-taught.

Artist statement

Thought my artistic creations I want to transmit to the public the problem that we face in society in order to find a solution. And I’m also inspired by a great HyperRealistic artist Arinze stanley , je said that: >_Most times it’s almost like I lose control of my pencil and the art flows through me to the paper. I work with my principle of the three p’s namely patience, practice and persistence. These have guided me over the years toward perfecting craft.
